# Gmail & Google Sheets

## Gmail Tools
- `gmail_search` - Tim email (Gmail search syntax)
- `gmail_read` - Doc noi dung 1 email (can message ID tu search)
- `gmail_archive` - Archive emails (xoa khoi inbox, khong xoa han)
- `gmail_trash` - Chuyen vao thung rac (xoa sau 30 ngay)
- `gmail_label` - Them/xoa labels cho emails
- `gmail_send` - Gui email moi
- `gmail_list_labels` - Liet ke tat ca labels

## Gmail search syntax
- `is:unread` - chua doc
- `from:user@example.com` - tu ai
- `subject:keyword` - tieu de chua
- `newer_than:2d` - trong 2 ngay gan
- `has:attachment` - co file dinh kem
- `label:name` - co label cu the
- Ket hop: `is:unread from:boss newer_than:7d`

## Google Sheets Tools
- `sheets_read` - Doc data tu sheet
- `sheets_write` - Ghi de data vao range
- `sheets_append` - Them rows vao cuoi sheet
- `sheets_list` - Liet ke tat ca tabs
- `sheets_create_tab` - Tao tab moi

- Khi don dep hang loat: bao so luong va loai email truoc, hoi xac nhan
